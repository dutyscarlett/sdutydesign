GRAPHIC DESIGN

ABOUT

Scarlett Duty is a graphic designer from Gainesville, Florida, pursuing a Bachelor of Fine Arts in graphic design with a mass communications minor at the University of Florida. Scarlett explores interactivity, materials, and technology in their work.

Scarlett is interested in branding, web design, typography, and illustration. Their process starts on paper before moving to digital media. During thier process, elements such as typography, color, layout, medium, and material are thoroughly explored. They utilize Adobe design programs, Figma, and Clip Studio.

In their spare time, Scarlett can be found reading, biking, birding, or hanging out with their cats, Hobo Joe and Phoebe.

Bigelow Rebrand

BrandingDesign

Bigelow is a family-owned tea brand established in 1945. They carry a variety of teas and flavors, and for this project, I focused on their Salted Caramel flavor. Rather than think inside or outside the box, I thought of the box. I explored how material influences and is influenced by design, pushing Bigelow's identity to new limits in this imaginary brand redesign.

Upgrade

BrandingWeb Design

Upgrade is a fictional online business that provides international tech repair services for outdated, obsolete, and vintage technology. This project explores how typography communicates brand identity and how a brandname can be simplified into a recognizable lettermark.

Plantstay

AdvertisingDesign

Plantsay is a locally-owned plant shop from Gainesville that opened in 2020. They sell a variety of ethically sourced houseplants, from common household finds to more exotic collectors' items.

Yearbook Zine

PrintDesign

Workshop for Art Research and Practice is a course at the University of Florida that has students push their boundaries in terms of creative thinking and artistic output. For several hours a week, my course section of 25 students met and worked in the same space. To honor our camaraderie and the bonds formed from our class, I made a small yearbook in the format of an eight-page zine.
