ABOUT

Scarlett Duty is a graphic designer from Gainesville, Florida, pursuing a Bachelor of Fine Arts in graphic design with a mass communications minor at the University of Florida. Scarlett explores interactivity, materials, and technology in their work.

Scarlett is interested in branding, web design, typography, and illustration. Their process starts on paper before moving to digital media. During thier process, elements such as typography, color, layout, medium, and material are thoroughly explored. They utilize Adobe design programs, Figma, and Clip Studio.

In their spare time, Scarlett can be found reading, biking, birding, or hanging out with their cats, Hobo Joe and Phoebe.

Bigelow Rebrand

BrandingDesign

Bigelow is a family-owned tea brand established in 1945. They carry a variety of teas and flavors, and for this project, I focused on their Salted Caramel flavor. Rather than think inside or outside the box, I thought of the box. I explored how material influences and is influenced by design, pushing Bigelow's identity to new limits.
